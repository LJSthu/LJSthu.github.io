Bio

Hi, Im Jiashuo! I'm a Research Scientist at ByteDance Seed, as a member of TopSeed Program, working with Wenhao Huang.
I keep a close connection to academia, working with Jose Blanchet, Peng Cui, Bo Li, Hongseok Namkoong, Mihaela van der Schaar, and Mengdi Wang. I got my Ph.D. from Tsinghua CS, and visited Stanford MS&E, Cambridge CCAIM, and Columbia DRO as a visiting student researcher.

Current: I focus on LLM Evaluation & Pretraining, including:
  • Efficient and reliable evaluation algorithms for LLMs
  • Super large-scale data-centric AI
  • Practical and fancy benchmarks for LLMs
I also lead the FutureX project, a live benchmark for LLM agents on real-world future prediction tasks, which has gained over 50 million views on X (twitter)!

Past: During my Ph.D. time, I develop robust, reliable, and fair machine learning models, with a particular focus on addressing distribution shifts and data-centric AI, and bridge the fields of machine learning, operations research, and healthcare. For an overview of my past research, please refer to our NeurIPS'23 Tutorial, SDM'24 Tutorial, CoLLAs'24 Tutorial, and KDD'25 Tutorial.

Intern: Zerui Cheng (Princeton), Jiayun Wu (CMU)
Long-term Collaborator: Nabeel Seedat (Cambridge)
