Bio

Hi, Im Jiashuo! I'm a final-year Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Computer Science & Technology at Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Peng Cui and Prof. Bo Li. I was a visiting student researcher at Prof. Jose H. Blanchet's group in the Department of Management Science & Engineering at Stanford University. I also work remotely with Prof. Hongseok Namkoong in the Decision, Risk, and Operations division at Columbia Business School. I am visiting Prof. Mihaela van der Schaar's group at Cambridge Centre for AI in Medicine.

My research lies at the interface of machine learning, operations research, and health-care applications, including:
(1) Generalization under Distribution Shifts: Theoretical foundations and tools for understanding real-world distribution shifts;
(2) Data Heterogeneity: Algorithms to model and exploit data heterogeneity under distribution shifts;
(3) Data-Driven Distributionally robust optimization: Addressing the over-pessimism problem in real applications.

Recently, I'm focusing on the robust optimization with/for LLMs as well as Health-Care applications.
